Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information necessary to provide our personal expense tracking service. This includes account registration details (such as your username and email address) and financial transaction records you manually input (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide, maintain, and improve your experience with Spendly. We process your transaction records to generate category breakdowns, spending patterns, and monthly summaries so you can effectively manage your finances.

3. Data Storage

All personal financial records and account information are stored securely in our database. We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable financial information to outside parties. Your financial data remains strictly private and accessible only through your authenticated account.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please feel free to reach out to our support team at support@spendly.local.
